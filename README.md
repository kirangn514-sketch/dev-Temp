## 💡 Tips for Interview Success

> **"You don't rise to the level of your goals, you fall to the level of your systems."**

1. **Practice Daily** — Solve at least 1 DSA problem per day
2. **Explain Out Loud** — Practice explaining concepts as if teaching
3. **Build Projects** — Apply every concept in a real mini-project
4. **Review Mistakes** — Keep a log of wrong answers and revisit them
5. **Mock Interviews** — Simulate pressure with timed sessions
6. **Security Mindset** — Always think about "what can go wrong"
